Banks
The Big 4 banks in the U.S. are JPMorgan Chase (AUM $3,307B), Bank of America (AUM $2,520B), Wells Fargo (AUM $1,780B), and Citibank (AUM $1,670B). Commercial banks generally make money in two ways:
  • Interest rates
  • Credit card debt
